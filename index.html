about us

fuel4school is a student-led effort to fix poor school lunches. we care about food justice and healthier meals.

the school lunch budget is $600 million, just 1.5% of the $40 billion doe budget. cuts have removed popular items like chicken tenders.

nyc schools produce 80 million pounds of trash yearly, with 47% as food waste. composting helps, but much is still wasted.
